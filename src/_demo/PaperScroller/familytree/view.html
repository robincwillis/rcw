Relationships

  • Lola Gibbs

    Dallas aviatrix and socialite known for her beauty and wit. Leland married and divorced her twice.

  • Slim Keith

    Hemingway’s muse and 20th century swan. Slim was as glamorous and as beautiful as any star. Instead of becoming one herself she tapped several including Lauren Bacall in “To Have and Have Not” casting her to play the role that her then husband Howard Hawks modeled on her. She always wore the most beautiful gold cuffs.

  • Margaret Brooke Sullavan

    An authentic southern belle, star of stage and screen married to Henry Fonda, and later Leland Hayward. Mother of Brooke, Bridget and Bill.

  • Pamela CHurchhill Hayward

    Married to Randolph Churchill son of Winston Churchill, and Leland’s 4th wife, went on to marry wartime lover Avereil Harriman.
